Support Our Mission

Thank you for your interest in supporting Tres Hermanas PR! Your donations help us build and maintain Little Free Libraries throughout Aguadilla, Puerto Rico, bringing books and the joy of reading to children in our community.

Nonprofit Status

We are excited to share that we have our non-profit status as a 501(c)(3). Our EIN number is: 39-4273416. Make a tax-deductible donation today!

How Your Donation Helps

Your generous contributions support:

  • Building materials for new Little Free Libraries
  • Shipping costs for books from the mainland United States to Puerto Rico
  • Maintenance and upkeep of existing libraries
  • Expansion to new locations throughout the community

Ways to Donate

There are several ways you can support our mission:

1. Financial Donations

Monetary donations help us cover shipping costs (approximately $20 per box), building materials, and ongoing maintenance. All donations are tax-deductible.

2. Book Donations

We accept gently used children's books in English and Spanish for ages baby through 6th grade. Learn more about how to donate books on our How to Participate page.

3. Building Supplies

If you have woodworking skills or building materials you'd like to donate, please contact us directly.

Contact Us

For more information about donating or to discuss larger contributions, please reach out to us. We're happy to answer any questions you may have.

You can also download our Donation Letter for more details about our project.

Join Our Movement

Sharing Books. Inspiring Young Minds in Puerto Rico.

At Tres Hermanas PR, we believe every child deserves the joy of discovering a great story. Our mission is simple: to nurture a love of reading by giving children across Puerto Rico access to books.

Through local community book drives and partnerships with schools and libraries in the States, we bring stories, imagination, and possibility to little readers en la Isla del Encanto.

Join us — donate, volunteer, and spread the word. Together, we're building a brighter future, one book — and one story — at a time.

Tres Hermanas PR is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. EIN: 39-4273416

Mission Statement

Our mission is to inspire a love of reading and community connection across Puerto Rico by providing children with access to books that reflect their culture, language, and imagination. Through fundraising, donations, and local partnerships, Tres Hermanas PR works to fill Little Libraries and community spaces with stories that empower young readers and celebrate the joy of learning.

Youth-Led Book Drives

Host a book drive at your school or in your community to help kids in Puerto Rico discover the joy of reading. We'll guide you every step of the way.

How Your Gift Helps

📦

Bulk Shipping

Funds go directly toward shipping books in bulk to the island, ensuring maximum impact for every dollar.

📚

Spanish-Language Books

Donations also help us purchase gently used Spanish-language books, which are harder to source through U.S. book drives.

🏗️

Little Free Libraries

Your support helps us build and maintain Little Free Libraries in communities across Puerto Rico.

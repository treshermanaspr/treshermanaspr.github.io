BOOK DRIVE

Help Kids in Puerto Rico!

Join Tres Hermanas PR in bringing books to children across Puerto Rico!

After Hurricane Maria, many children lost access to books and libraries. Your book donations help us stock Little Free Libraries and community spaces where kids can discover the joy of reading.

What We're Collecting:

📚 Books We Need:

  • Picture books (ages 0-8)
  • Early readers (ages 5-8)
  • Chapter books (ages 8-12)
  • Spanish-language books
  • Board books for toddlers
  • Educational workbooks

❌ Please No:

  • Damaged or torn books
  • Adult novels
  • Textbooks
  • Books with writing in them
  • Water-damaged books
  • Outdated reference books

📚 BOOK DRIVE 📚

Help Kids in Puerto Rico Get Books!

WE NEED YOUR BOOKS!

Join us in bringing books to children across Puerto Rico!

After Hurricane Maria, many children lost access to books and libraries. Your book donations help us stock Little Free Libraries where kids can discover the joy of reading.

📖 What We're Collecting 📖

📚 Books We Need:

  • Picture books (ages 0-8)
  • Early readers (ages 5-8)
  • Chapter books (ages 8-12)
  • Spanish-language books (all ages)
  • Board books for toddlers

❌ Please No:

  • Books with torn or missing pages
  • Water damaged books
  • Adult novels or textbooks
  • Books with writing/highlighting
  • Outdated reference books
  • Books in poor condition
  • Religious Books

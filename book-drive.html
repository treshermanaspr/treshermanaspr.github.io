Start Your Own Book Drive

Lead a book collection at your school or in your community to help kids in Puerto Rico

We're here to guide you every step of the way

Why Book Drives Matter

📚

Direct Impact

Every book you collect goes directly to children in Puerto Rico who need access to reading materials.

💪

Youth Leadership

You'll develop leadership skills while making a real difference in the lives of other young people.

🌍

Community Building

Book drives bring people together around the shared value of literacy and education.

Be Part of Something Pretty Cool

We want to invite you to send books across the United States and over the Atlantic Ocean to put them in Little Free Libraries we are building in Puerto Rico. Organize a small book drive. You can go to your class, your Girl/Boy Scout Troop, your parents' place of work, even your public library. Tell them about your interest in this project and would they be interested too? If yes - you are only a little bit of work away from giving a kid a book.

How to Start Your Book Drive

1

Plan Your Drive

Choose your location (school, community center, workplace) and set a timeline. Most drives run for 2-4 weeks.

  • Get permission from school/organization administrators
  • Choose collection dates and locations
  • Set a goal (number of books to collect)
2

Spread the Word

Create flyers, make announcements, and use social media to let people know about your book drive.

  • Use our printable flyer template
  • Make announcements at school/community meetings
  • Share on social media with #TresHermanasPR
3

Collect Books

Set up collection boxes and gather gently used children's books in English and Spanish.

  • Focus on books for ages baby to 6th grade
  • Accept books in good condition
  • Sort and count as you go
4

Ship to Puerto Rico

Package your books and ship them to our Puerto Rico address. We'll help with shipping costs!

  • Use sturdy boxes for shipping
  • Contact us for shipping assistance
  • Track your shipment
5

Celebrate Your Impact

Share your success story and inspire others to start their own book drives.

  • Take photos of your book collection
  • Share your story on social media
  • Inspire others to start drives

What Books to Collect

✅ Great Books to Collect

  • Picture books (ages 0-8)
  • Early readers (ages 5-8)
  • Chapter books (ages 8-12)
  • Spanish-language books (all ages)
  • Educational workbooks
  • Board books for toddlers

❌ Books to Avoid

  • Books with torn or missing pages
  • Water damaged books
  • Adult novels or textbooks
  • Books with writing/highlighting
  • Outdated reference books
  • Books in poor condition
